## Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Ensure you're an admin in the group">
    Ensure you're an admin of the group the bot will be added to and have the necessary permissions to give @HyperRaidBot full permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add @HyperRaidBot to Group">
    [Click Here](https://t.me/HyperRaidBot?startgroup=true&admin) to add @HyperRaidBot to your TG Group with admin permissions.
    <Info>Alternatively, add the bot manually by the username @HyperRaidBot. </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Promote bot to admin">
    Once added, promote @HyperRaidBot to admin if not already done. Ensure the bot has at least the following permissions:
    - Delete messages
    - Send messages
    - Pin messages
    - Any other permissions you want the bot to have
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a Raid">
    Send `/raid [TWEET_URL]` in your group to start and configure your first raid!
    <Tip>Example: `/raid https://x.com/mellow0x69/status/1810329069784793095`</Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
