## Change Media in `/settings`
In the `/settings` page, on the `Raid Media` tab, you can change the media that is shown above the raid message depending on the status of the raid.

For example:
- **"Raid Started"**: Show a video on top of the raid message when the raid is started.
- **"Raid Executing"**: Show a video on top of the raid message when the raid is executing.
- **"Raid Finished"**: Show a video on top of the raid message when the raid is finished.

<Info>These settings apply to all raid types (Standard Raids, AutoRaids, SmartReplies)</Info>
