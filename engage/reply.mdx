## Replying to a Raid
1. Press the `[ðŸ’¬]` button at the bottom of the Raid message. This will open the Reply page.
2. Type your reply in the text box.
3. Hit `[Quick Reply]` to send your reply.

### Adding Images to your Reply
1. If you want to add images to your reply, press the `[Add Media]` button.
2. Upload images by clicking the `[Upload Image]` button.
3. Once the images are uploaded, press on the images (up to 4) to add them to your reply.
4. Press the `[Add Selected]` button to add the selected images to your reply.
5. Press the `[Quick Reply]` button to send your reply.

<Info>
  It may take a few seconds for a reply to be sent if images are included.
</Info>
