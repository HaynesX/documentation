### Engage on Raids

You can engage on raids by clicking the buttons in the TG chat.
- **Like**: Like the tweet being raided.
- **Retweet**: Retweet the tweet being raided.
- **Bookmark**: Bookmark the tweet being raided.
- **Reply**: Reply to the tweet being raided.
- **Smash**: Automatically like, retweet, bookmark all at once. (Excludes replies).
