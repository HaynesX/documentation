### Add a KOL via `/autoraid`
1. Use the `/autoraid` command to open the AutoRaid page.
2. Click the `[⚙️ Configure AutoRaid]` button.
3. Click the `[Add KOL]` button.
4. Enter the X @Username handle of the KOL you want to add.
5. Click the `[Add]` button.
6. The KOL will be added to your AutoRaid list.
7. The bot will now scan the KOL's account every minute for new Tweets.
