### Enable AutoRaid via `/autoraid`
1. Use the `/autoraid` command to open the AutoRaid page.
2. Click the `[⚙️ Configure AutoRaid]` button.
3. Click the `[Settings]` tab.
4. Enable AutoRaid via the Toggle.
5. Once Toggled, the bot will now scan the KOL's account every minute for new Tweets.

<Info>
  It may take a few minutes for the bot to start scanning the KOL's account.
</Info>
