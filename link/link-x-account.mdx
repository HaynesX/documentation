## Link X Account via `/link`
Once on the `/link` page, you can authorize HyperRaidBot to use your X Account by clicking the `Authorize on X` button and following the instructions.

### Instructions

1. Type `/link` within a Telegram Group that has HyperRaidBot installed.
2. Click the `ðŸ”— Link / Manage X Account` button.
3. Click the `Authorize on X` button.
4. Login on X, and authorize HyperRaidBot to use your X Account.
5. Once authorized, you'll be redirected back to the `/link` page where you can see the X Account that was linked.
6. You are now able to engage on raids! ðŸŽ‰

<Info>
You can also link more than one X Account to your Telegram account.
</Info>
